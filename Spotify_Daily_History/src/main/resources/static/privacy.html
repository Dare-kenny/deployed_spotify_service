Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

1. Information We Collect

Spotify Daily History only collects the information provided by Spotify after you log in with your Spotify account, such as your display name, email address, and listening history (today’s tracks).

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To display your daily listening history (tracks played, play counts, and times).
  • To personalize your experience within this app only.
  • We never share or sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

No permanent storage is used. Your Spotify data is fetched in real time when you log in and is not saved to any external database beyond your active session.

4. Third-Party Services

This app uses the official Spotify Web API. Please review Spotify’s own Privacy Policy for details on how they handle your account and listening data.

5. Your Rights

You may revoke this app’s access to your Spotify account at any time from your Spotify Account Settings.

6. Contact

If you have any concerns, please contact us at 2005darekenny@gmail.com.
